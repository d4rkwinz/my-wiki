**When does something belong here vs. elsewhere?**

- **Here** — material studying *how a business operates* (pricing, positioning, growth, moats, founder decisions). Source-shaped, useful as-is, comparative.
- [[notes/ai/index|ai]] — material about AI tools and workflows.
- [[notes/repos/index|repos]] — GitHub bookmarks (one-liners).
- `wiki/concepts/` — atomic business concepts that compose with other concepts (e.g. `Product-Market Fit`, `Bottom-Up GTM`). Promote via the normal raw → digest → wiki pipeline if/when those emerge.
